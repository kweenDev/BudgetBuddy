Core Features

Multi-Category Tracking

Track expenses across various categories like Food, Transport, Utilities, and more.

Monthly Summaries

Savings Goals

Budget Comparison

How It Works

Step 1: Install

Clone the repository, install dependencies, and get started with the CLI using python main.py.

Step 2: Set Your Budget

Define your budget for the month and categorise your spending limits.

Step 3: Track Expenses

Add your expenses under the appropriate categories and monitor your financial health.

Step 4: Analyse and Adjust

View monthly summaries, adjust your budget, and reach your savings goals faster.
